EHR Digital Correspondence.

Various studies have highlighted that administrative duties related to Electronic Health Records (EHR) significantly contribute to provider burnout. Among these tasks, managing the EHR Inbox stands out as particularly challenging for many providers. Research indicates that providers dedicate over an hour each day to address Inbox items. Additionally, a study revealed that one medical institution had accumulated a staggering 18 million unread Inbox items within their system.

INFOMEDEX's Inbox Management Solution empowers providers to efficiently handle their Inbox tasks by automating the processing of certain items based on predefined protocols and directing others to the appropriate responsible party. Combining INFOMEDEX's advanced technology with clinical expertise, this solution can alleviate over 50% of the Inbox burden for provider enterprises. Comprehensive in scope, INFOMEDEX's Inbox Management Solution streamlines Inbox management for all members of the care team. By reducing response times to messages, it enhances the patient experience and effectively addresses provider burnout.
