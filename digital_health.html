Healthcare Information.

The healthcare industry faces significant pressure to deliver improved, safer, and more efficient care, all while experiencing a decline in the number of providers and an increasing demand from patients for a consumer-centric experience. Empowering care providers to operate at the peak of their licensure, while furnishing them with personalized insights precisely when they're making critical decisions, is essential to tackling these challenges head-on.

Digital Health solutions represent a range of offerings aimed at tackling these challenges.
1. Care Intelligence – Platform Approach & Data Driven INFOMEDEX Health’s Unified Data Platform (UDP) processes and organizes complex healthcare data and additional relevant data sources to aid in care decisions. This process simplifies data management and fosters innovation by standardizing data elements for use in various solutions. UDP is flexible, allowing for the seamless integration of intelligence into care workflows. The normalized data can be stored in different formats to support diverse use cases such as real-time solutions, research, data science, and analytics.

2. Process Automation – Process Automation through software programs (Bots) replicates high-volume human tasks with predefined logic. We offer existing solutions and can develop new ones as needed, requiring only appropriate system access, similar to human users (credentials and functionality). INFOMEDEX Health provides the infrastructure and ongoing maintenance.

3. Digital Automation – Product Engineering (Development & Enhancements) – INFOMEDEX Health utilizes diverse product development teams to collaborate with clients on creating new capabilities and solutions. These offerings address gaps in existing technology or enhance current solutions. INFOMEDEX Health undertakes this process at scale, providing full development and maintenance services for existing vendors in the market.
